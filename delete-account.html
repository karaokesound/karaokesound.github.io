Delete Your Account

You can delete your BakeBuddy account and all associated data directly from within the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

In-App Instructions

1 Open BakeBuddy Launch the app on your device
2 Go to Profile Tap the Profile tab in the navigation
4 Delete Account Tap "Usuń konto" and confirm

Upon deletion, your account, recipes, and all personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

or

Contact Support

If you're unable to access the app or prefer to request deletion via email, reach out to our support team and we'll process your request within 7 days.

Send your deletion request to

service.bakebuddy@gmail.com

Please include in your email

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
